The Lake My favorite place in the world is the lake. No matter what is happening in my life, if I am sitting in a boat on the lake I am in my happy place. Better yet, if I am behind the boat riding a wakeboard or surfing, I am even happier. Something about the water and the surroundings that are just so peaceful. I fell in love with wakeboarding when my family got a boat about 5 years ago. There is just so much adrenaline when I am in the air. It is like everything is in slow motion. I look at my family and friends in the boat, the other cool boats, and the water. The best thing is when the speakers are blaring a good song when you're on a good run. My parents made me stop for a while because last summer I tore my labrum wakeboarding. This sucked, but this is when I also discovered wake surfing. Like surfing, you ride the wave only this kind of surfing, the wave is created by the boat. It is pretty cool hurts much less. You cannot do as many tricks, but it is fun riding behind the boat without holding onto anything.
Atlanta Falcons Games I have grown up watching the Atlanta Falcons which has led them to become my favorite sports team. The sport of football is exciting to watch, but even more exciting when you have a team to root for. Even though they continue to disappoint me and crush my spirit with the endless amount of false hope, I will continue to cheer them on.

A bit more about my travels.

While my friends and I took our road trip, we had the chance to take a trip down the Grand Canyon. We hit the Rim trail, the Bright Angel Trail, and the South Kaibab Trail. It was a heck of an experience because of the enormous landscape. My eyes could not even process the monstrosity of the landscape. One weekend, earlier in my college career, I made a trip to Montana for the weekend to go hiking and go skiing. It was an incredible experience nevertheless, but I will definitely stretch out that drive longer the next time we embark on such a trip.
